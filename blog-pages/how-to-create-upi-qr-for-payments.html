How to Create a UPI QR Code for Payments

By QIFY • Step-by-Step Guide • 6 min read

A UPI QR code lets people pay you quickly by scanning a code with apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, and other UPI apps. It is one of the easiest ways to receive payments for shops, small businesses, freelancers, and personal use.

In this guide, you will learn how to create a UPI QR code for payments in a simple way.

What you need before creating a UPI QR code

  • A bank account linked to UPI.
  • A UPI ID or virtual payment address.
  • A payment app like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or BHIM.

How to create a UPI QR code

  1. Open your UPI app.
  2. Go to the QR code or Receive payment section.
  3. Select your bank account or UPI ID.
  4. Choose the option to create or share a QR code.
  5. Save the QR code or download it as an image.
  6. Print it, share it, or place it where customers can scan it easily.

How it works after someone scans it

When a customer scans your UPI QR code, the payment app opens your payment details automatically. The payer enters the amount and approves the payment with their UPI PIN. The money is then sent directly to your linked bank account.

Best uses for UPI QR codes

  • Shop counters and billing desks
  • Restaurants and cafés
  • Freelance service payments
  • Small business collections
  • Personal payment requests

Tips for using your QR code safely

Always make sure your QR code is linked to the correct account. Check payment notifications carefully, and never share your UPI PIN with anyone. A QR code is for receiving money only.

Final words

Creating a UPI QR code is fast and simple. Once set up, it makes receiving payments easier for both you and your customers. It is a smart tool for modern digital payments.
