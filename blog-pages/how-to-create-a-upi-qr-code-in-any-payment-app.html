How to Create a UPI QR Code in Any Payment App

By QIFY • Step-by-Step Guide • 4 min read

UPI QR codes make digital payments simple and fast. Most payment apps today allow users to generate their own QR code for receiving money instantly.

In this guide, you will learn how to create a UPI QR code using popular apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and BHIM.

What is a UPI QR code?

A UPI QR code is a scannable payment code linked to your UPI ID or bank account. Customers can scan the code using any UPI app and send money directly to you.

What do you need before starting?

  • A smartphone with a payment app installed
  • A bank account linked to UPI
  • A verified mobile number
  • An active internet connection

General steps to create a UPI QR code

  1. Open your payment app.
  2. Go to the “Receive Money” or “QR Code” section.
  3. Select your bank account or UPI ID.
  4. Tap on “My QR” or “Generate QR Code”.
  5. Save or download the QR image.
  6. Share or print the QR code for payments.

How to create a QR code in Google Pay

  1. Open Google Pay.
  2. Tap your profile picture.
  3. Select “QR Codes” or “Show QR”.
  4. Save or share the QR code.

How to create a QR code in PhonePe

  1. Open PhonePe.
  2. Go to the “My QR” section.
  3. Your payment QR code will appear automatically.
  4. Download or share it.

How to create a QR code in Paytm

  1. Open Paytm.
  2. Go to “Accept Payments” or “My QR”.
  3. View your UPI QR code.
  4. Save or print it for customers.

How to create a QR code in BHIM

  1. Open the BHIM app.
  2. Go to the profile or QR section.
  3. Select your payment QR code.
  4. Save or share the QR image.

Best ways to use your UPI QR code

  • Print it for your shop counter
  • Add it to invoices and bills
  • Share it on WhatsApp or social media
  • Use it for freelance payments
  • Place it in cafés or restaurants

Helpful tip

Always test your QR code before using it publicly. Make sure the payment opens the correct account and receiver name.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Using the wrong linked account
  • Printing a blurry QR code
  • Sharing outdated QR codes
  • Not checking payment notifications

Final words

Creating a UPI QR code in any payment app is simple and takes only a few minutes. Once created, it makes receiving digital payments easier, faster, and more convenient.
