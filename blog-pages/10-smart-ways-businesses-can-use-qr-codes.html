10 Smart Ways Businesses Can Use QR Codes

By QIFY • Business Tips • 7 min read

QR codes are now used by businesses everywhere because they are simple, fast, and easy for customers to use. A single scan can open websites, make payments, show menus, connect to WiFi, and much more.

Here are 10 smart ways businesses can use QR codes to improve customer experience and grow faster.

1. Accept Digital Payments

Businesses can place UPI QR codes at counters or tables so customers can pay instantly using apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm.

2. Share Digital Menus

Restaurants and cafés can use QR codes to open digital menus on customer phones. This reduces printing costs and makes updating menus easier.

3. Connect Customers to WiFi

Hotels, cafés, and waiting areas can use WiFi QR codes so guests connect without typing passwords manually.

4. Promote Websites and Online Stores

QR codes can instantly direct users to a business website, product page, or online store from posters, packaging, or business cards.

5. Collect Customer Reviews

Businesses can place QR codes on bills or packaging that open Google review pages or feedback forms. This helps increase customer reviews quickly.

6. Share Contact Information

QR codes can store phone numbers, email addresses, social media links, and digital business cards for quick sharing.

7. Run Promotions and Discounts

Businesses can use QR codes to offer discount coupons, limited-time deals, or special offers to customers.

8. Improve Product Packaging

Product QR codes can open instructions, tutorials, warranty details, ingredients, or product videos.

9. Share Event Information

Event organizers can use QR codes for tickets, schedules, registration forms, and location directions.

10. Increase Social Media Engagement

QR codes can open Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, or WhatsApp pages directly and help businesses gain more followers.

Why businesses prefer QR codes

  • Easy for customers to use
  • Works with almost every smartphone
  • Low-cost marketing solution
  • Improves customer convenience
  • Saves time for both businesses and customers

Helpful tip

Always test your QR codes before printing them publicly. Make sure they are easy to scan and linked to the correct destination.

Final words

QR codes are powerful tools for modern businesses. Whether you want to accept payments, promote products, improve customer experience, or increase engagement, QR codes make everything faster and easier.
