How to Create an Email QR Code

Generate QR codes for email addresses using QIFY. Users can scan the QR code to instantly open their email app with your email address already filled in.

Step 1

Open the Email QR Generator tool on QIFY.

Enter your email address carefully.

Example: example@gmail.com

Step 2

After Entered Email Just click Generate QR button

Then click on the Generate QR button.

Step 3

Download the QR code image to your device.

Users can scan the QR code using any smartphone camera to instantly open their email app and contact you quickly.
