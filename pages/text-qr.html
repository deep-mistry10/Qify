How to Create a Text QR Code

Generate QR codes for plain text, messages, notes, instructions or any custom text using QIFY.

Step 1

Open the Text QR Generator tool on QIFY.

Type or paste the text you want inside the text input section.

You can add: messages, notes, codes, or any custom information.

Step 2

Check the entered text carefully.

Then click on the Generate QR button to create your QR code instantly.

Step 3

Download the QR code image to your device.

Users can scan the QR code using any smartphone camera or QR scanner app to instantly read the text.
