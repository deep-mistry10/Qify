How to Create a Contact QR Code

Generate QR codes for contacts, phone numbers, emails and personal details using QIFY. Users can scan the QR code and save your contact instantly.

Step 1

Open the Contact QR Generator tool on QIFY.

Enter your contact information such as:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address

Step 2

Check all entered details carefully before generating the QR code.

Then click on the Generate QR button.

Step 3

Download the QR code image to your device.

Anyone can scan the QR code using a smartphone camera to save your contact instantly.
