About Qify

Qify is a free online QR code generator built to help users create QR codes instantly without signup or complicated steps. Our tool allows users to generate multiple types of QR codes including URL, Text, WiFi, Contact, Mobile Number, Email, and UPI payment QR codes.

The goal of Qify is to provide a fast, simple, and privacy-friendly QR code generator that works on both desktop and mobile devices. Whether you are a student, shop owner, business owner, cyber cafe operator, or regular internet user, Qify helps you create QR codes easily in seconds.

Features of Qify

  • Create QR codes for URLs and websites
  • Generate dynamic-style UPI payment QR codes
  • Create WiFi QR codes for instant connection
  • Generate contact and phone QR codes
  • Free to use with no registration required
  • Mobile-friendly and easy to use

Privacy & Security

We care about user privacy. Qify does not store your sensitive information on any server. QR codes are generated directly in your browser, which means your data remains on your device.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make QR technology simple and accessible for everyone. We aim to build useful free online tools that are fast, lightweight, and user-friendly.
