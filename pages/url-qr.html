How to Create a URL QR Code

Generate QR codes for websites, webpages, YouTube videos, social media links and more using QIFY.

Step 1

Open the URL QR Generator tool on QIFY.

Copy and paste your website link inside the URL input section.

Example: https://example.com

Step 2

Click on the Generate QR button.

QIFY will instantly create a QR code for your website link.

Step 3

Download the QR code image to your device.

Users can scan the QR code using any smartphone camera or QR scanner app to open the link instantly.
