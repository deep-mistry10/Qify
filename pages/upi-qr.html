How to Generate UPI QR Code ?

Learn how to create payment QR codes for Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm and all UPI supported apps using QIFY.

Step 1

Enter your name inside the name field.

Now enter your UPI ID carefully.

Then enter the payment amount if you want a fixed amount payment QR code.

Step 2

Generate and download the QR code.

Anyone can scan the QR using any UPI app to send money instantly.
