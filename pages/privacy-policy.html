PRIVACY POLICY

Your Privacy Matters

This Privacy Policy explains how QIFY handles user information while using our QR code generator tools.

Information We Collect

QIFY does not require users to create an account or provide personal information to generate QR codes.

Most QR codes are generated directly inside your browser, which means your data stays on your device.

QR Code Data

We do not permanently store the QR code data you generate using our tools.

Any information entered into the generator, such as URLs, WiFi details, or UPI IDs, is processed temporarily to create the QR code.

Cookies and Local Storage

QIFY may use basic cookies or local storage to improve website performance, remember preferences, and enhance user experience.

Third-party services such as analytics or advertising providers may also use cookies according to their own policies.

Third-Party Services

QIFY may use third-party services including analytics tools, content delivery networks, fonts, or advertising platforms to improve the website experience.

These services may collect non-personal browser or device information.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect our website and users from unauthorized access or misuse.

However, no internet service can guarantee complete security.

Children's Privacy

QIFY is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time without prior notice.

Users are encouraged to review this page regularly.

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the contact page.
